WebRTC: Passing SDP Without Signaling

Follow the instructions below to pass SDP offer and answer between two tabs.

User 1 Video

User 2 Video

Step 1: User 1, click "Create Offer" to generate an SDP offer and copy it from the text area below.

Step 2: User 2, paste the SDP offer generated by User 1 into the text area above. Then click "Create Answer" to generate an SDP answer and copy it from the text area below.

Step 3: User 1, paste the SDP answer generated by User 2 into the text area above and then click "Add Answer".
